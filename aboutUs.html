About Us

Founded in 2006 in Johor Bahru, Inspire University is a private educational institution dedicated to providing quality education. Established by professional educators, we offer a wide range of programs through our faculties, including Mathematics & Science, Hospitality Management, Accountancy, and Applied Sciences, with diploma and degree options.

As our student population grew, we expanded to new campuses in Melaka and Selangor by 2012. In 2014, we launched an e-Learning platform to enhance online learning, making education more interactive and accessible.

To support our students further, we introduced the e-Dormitory Management System (e-DMS) in 2015, allowing on-campus residents to manage their accommodation easily. Today, Inspire University is home to over 5,000 students from across Malaysia, offering opportunities for academic growth and partnerships for master's and PhD programs.
